DESIGN SYSTEM DIRECTIVES

Master brand style guidelines.

Review the architectural guidelines constructed to govern the AURA identity system. This page serves as a model of the asset packs provided to our enterprise clients.

Visual System Integrity

To preserve visual consistency, all marketing collateral, UI platforms, packaging lines, and print publications must align with the layout grids and spacing tokens outlined below. No modifications to logo forms, font heights, or color codes are permitted.

If you are a vendor or developer implementing these assets, please coordinate with AURA's headshot team to request access to the asset repository containing clean SVG files.

LOGO APPLICATION

Clear Space & Prohibitions

01 / Clear Space Rule

The AURA logo mark requires a minimum exclusion zone of X-height (equivalent to the height of the letter 'A' in the mark) on all sides. Do not allow surrounding icons, banners to intersect this perimeter.

CORRECT USE

AURA

The logo must render in flat black (#111827) on light backgrounds, or white (#FFFFFF) on dark surfaces. The dot must maintain the creative purple accent color.

PROHIBITED USE

A U R A

Do not stretch characters, skew vertical bounds, change font styles, or apply color gradients to the primary letterforms.

TYPOGRAPHY SYSTEM

Text Scales & Families

Heading spec: Sora
A B C D E F G
Headings must maintain a tracking letter space of -0.03em and 1.15 line height. Use Sora Bold (700) for hero lines and Sora SemiBold (600) for card titles.
Body spec: Inter
a b c d e f g
Body paragraphs require Inter Light or Regular (300/400). Maintain a tracking space of 0em and a line height of 1.65 to 1.75 to guarantee readability.
