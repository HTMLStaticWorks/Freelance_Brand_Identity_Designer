A comprehensive visual identity project (discovery, color strategy, typography pairing, logo systems, and style guide delivery) typically spans 4 to 6 weeks. Simple logo revisions or identity updates can be completed in 3 weeks.

We focus on brand strategy, visual direction, and high-fidelity front-end designs (HTML/CSS templates). We coordinate directly with your software engineering team, supplying them with clean Figma layouts, semantic code blocks, CSS variables, and organized SVG vector libraries.

Project fees are structured on a milestone basis: 50% deposit to confirm dates and initiate strategy audits, and 50% upon final delivery of all approved branding packages, guides, and typography files.

Once the final milestone invoice is settled, full intellectual property and copyrights for the logos, visual files, guidelines, and templates transfer completely to the client. Font licenses are registered to the client's entity directly during purchase.
