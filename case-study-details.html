CASE STUDY / REBRAND

Aetheria Logistics — Restructuring a global shipping identity.

Client
Aetheria Global
Services
Logo, Typography, Strategy
Timeline
6 Weeks (2026)
Core Focus
High-Contrast Scales

The Challenge

Aetheria operates across 40 countries, yet their original visual asset suite felt cluttered, outdated, and struggled with scaling. Web apps loaded slowly due to heavy non-vector assets, and print collateral printed with color discrepancies. The founder required a clean, scalable, editorial system that conveyed modern security.

The Design Strategy

We abandoned their complex, multi-colored globe logo and engineered a custom, high-contrast serif wordmark paired with an abstract line emblem. We defined a strict layout grid, a neutral light mode scheme with creative purples as highlights, and optimized all vector elements for rapid loading times.

Project Deliverables

  • ✓ Master Vector Wordmark
  • ✓ Accessible Contrast Color Tokens
  • ✓ Custom Brand Guideline Portal
  • ✓ Front-End CSS Theme Tokens
  • ✓ Digital Packaging Mockups
