Meet Aura Thorne.

I am an independent brand identity designer specializing in typographic architecture, responsive logo systems, and WCAG-compliant accessibility guidelines. I collaborate directly with tech startups and forward-thinking founders to establish clear, authoritative visual identities.

By treating visual assets as rigorous, logical systems rather than subjective decoration, I eliminate design inconsistencies and ensure seamless handoffs to developers. My process bridges the gap between artistic integrity and commercial growth strategy.
